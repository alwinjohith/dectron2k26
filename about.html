WELCOME TO THE ARENA

Dectron'26 is not just a symposium; it's a high-stakes arena where the brightest minds come to bet on their own innovation. This year, we've stripped away the corporate polish and embraced the thrill of the gamble with our theme: The Wicked Jester.

Every line of code written, every circuit designed, and every strategy formed is a card played in our grand casino. We believe that true progress comes from calculated risks and the courage to double down on your ideas when the chips are down.

"In the house of technology, the only way to beat the dealer is to become the architect of a new game. Dectron'26 is your seat at the table."

Hosted by the Electronics and Communication Department, Dectron'26 features two days of intense competition across technical and non-technical domains. Whether you're aiming for the jackpot in our hackathon or bluffing your way through our social games, the experience is designed to be wicked, thrilling, and unforgettable.
